Origin Realms Guide

Cosmetics

Get ready to express yourself and stand out from the crowd with our awesome collection of wearable cosmetics, gestures, and tool skins. From trendy hats to epic dance moves, we'll show you how to customize your character and make a statement in the game.

Wearable Cosmetics

With 4 different types, wearable cosmetics can be worn on your character as a great way to express yourself. Once you've gotten your hands on a cosmetic token, head over to Cosmo’s Cosmetics and talk to Cosmo.

Once you have redeemed your first cosmetics, step into the Wardrobe to begin customizing your character! Here you’ll see different tabs for the 4 cosmetic types: Hats, Backswags, Props, and Balloons. Cosmetics you own have a green background and can be equipped by clicking on them, and selecting what color you’d like it to be if the cosmetic is paintable.

Once you’ve gotten your hands on a Gesture Token, head over to Jester’s Gestures at the Origin Isles Carnival. By holding the Token and talking to Jester, you can redeem the token to gain access to that specific Gesture. If you ever wish to withdraw the token, talk to Jester again and shift-click the Gesture you want to withdraw.

Player in Cosmo's Wardrobe

Gestures are accessed by typing /g anywhere in the world, which brings up a menu of your favorite Gestures. You can change those options however you like, and access the full list of Gestures you have access to from this menu.

Tool Skins

Tool skins are cosmetics you can apply to your existing tools to give them a fresh new look and particle effects, while still keeping all of the properties of the actual tool you’ve placed the skin on.

Once you have obtained a tool skin, you can take it to Boulder and apply it via the forging menu. Tool skins can be placed on any tool regardless of its kind and it will adapt the qualities of that tool, including Relic tools. You can remove skins from tools via Boulder also.
